Building a Narrative

In Navqor, a narrative is a sequence of entangled superpositions that convey actions, emotions, and relationships.

Sentence 1: (Q₁ + Q₃ + Q₇) entangled with (Q₂ + Q₄ + Q₇) → "I move with you while we stay connected"
Sentence 2: (Q₁ + Q₅) entangled with (Q₂ + Q₆ + Q₈) → "I grow as the other decays and separates"
Sequence = a short story of interdependent experiences

Each "sentence" in a narrative can collapse differently depending on context or listener perspective.

Poetic Techniques

  • Repetition: Repeat superpositions to emphasize ritualistic or rhythmic meaning.
  • Parallel entanglement: Use multiple entangled entities in a mirrored structure for poetic balance.
  • Layered collapse: Intentionally allow ambiguous superpositions to collapse differently for multiple interpretations.
(Q₁ + Q₅ + Q₇) repeated 3x → "Growth and connection emphasized across time"
(Q₁ + Q₆ + Q₈) entangled with (Q₂ + Q₆ + Q₈) → "Shared decay and separation, echoed poetically"

Summary

  • Navqor narratives combine multiple states and entanglements to convey interdependent experiences.
  • Poetic techniques such as repetition, parallel entanglement, and layered collapse enhance expressive power.
  • Storytelling in Navqor is flexible: meaning depends on context, listener, and interpretation of collapse.
